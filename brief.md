# GraphRAG YouTube History — Scope

## Core Problem
- Users watch YouTube videos to learn, but cannot later find or revisit specific ideas, explanations, or insights they have seen.

---

## Goal
- Transform a user's YouTube watch history into a searchable, structured knowledge system.
- Enable users to search their watched videos and retrieve the exact segments where information appears.

---

## Core Requirements

### 1. Watch History Import
- Users can import their YouTube watch history.
- Option to define a starting point (initial sync date) for the system.

---

### 2. Search Across Content
- Users can perform natural language searches across all watched videos.

---

### 3. Retrieval Output
- Search results include:
  - Video link
  - Timestamp
  - Matched text snippet (from transcript)

---

### 4. Sync & Updates
- Configurable sync options:
  - Manual
  - Daily
  - Weekly
- Sync updates the list of watched videos and metadata.

---

### 5. Grounded Results
- All results are strictly from the user's watch history.
- No external content is included.
